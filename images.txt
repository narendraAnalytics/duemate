SMART INVOICE CREATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  Description
  ───────────
  Create detailed invoices with line items, GST rates, discounts
  (flat or percentage), and taxes — all computed live before saving.
  A full preview modal shows the final invoice layout before it's
  committed to the database.

  Problem Solved
  ──────────────
  Business owners waste time writing invoices by hand or in
  spreadsheets with no preview, leading to errors they only catch
  after sending.

  How It Works
  ────────────
  • Select a saved buyer → fields auto-fill (name, shop, GSTIN)
  • Add line items from your saved products catalogue
  • Set discount type (flat ₹ or %), GST/tax rate
  • Click "Preview Invoice" → review full breakdown
  • Click "Save Invoice" → stock quantities auto-decrement,
    buyer receives an email notification instantly

  Key Details
  ───────────
  • Subtotal → Discount → After-Discount → Tax → Total
  • Invoice number auto-generated
  • Supports INR and other currencies
  • Free plan: 4 invoices/month
