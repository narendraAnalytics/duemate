  WHAT IS DUEMATE?
  ────────────────
  DueMate is an AI-powered invoicing and payment reminder SaaS
  built for small and medium businesses in India. It solves one
  of the most persistent problems in small business operations:
  getting paid on time.

  Most small businesses — traders, wholesalers, manufacturers,
  freelancers — create invoices manually, track payments in
  notebooks or spreadsheets, and chase buyers through awkward
  phone calls or messages. Reminders get forgotten. Payments get
  delayed. Cash flow suffers.

  DueMate automates the entire payment collection cycle — from
  the moment an invoice is created to the day it's paid — so
  business owners never have to manually chase a payment again.

  THE CORE IDEA
  ─────────────
  Create an invoice → buyer gets notified instantly.
  Record a payment → buyer gets a receipt automatically.
  Invoice goes overdue → buyer gets a reminder every day at
  11 AM without you lifting a finger.

  All of this happens in the background, reliably, even if
  you close the app.

  WHO IS IT FOR?
  ──────────────
  · Wholesale traders managing dozens of buyers each month
  · Small manufacturers billing on credit terms (net 15/30)
  · Service businesses that invoice clients regularly
  · Anyone tired of asking "did you get my invoice?" manually

  HOW IT STANDS APART
  ────────────────────
  · Zero manual follow-up — reminders run on autopilot daily
  · Smart email cap — free plan users always get their overdue
    reminder delivered, even with a 3-email/buyer/month limit
  · Buyer & supplier memory — save once, reuse forever
  · Live stock tracking — inventory decrements on every sale
  · Built for India — GST rates, GSTIN validation, INR default,
    HSN codes, and Indian number formatting throughout
  · No bloat — create an invoice in under 60 seconds

  TECHNOLOGY BACKBONE
  ───────────────────
  · Inngest   — durable background jobs for reminders
                (survives server restarts, never drops a job)
  · Resend    — transactional email delivery with audit log
  · Gemini    — AI invoice data extraction (Plus/Pro plans)
  · WhatsApp  — Meta Cloud API reminders (Plus/Pro plans)
  · Neon      — serverless PostgreSQL for instant scale
  · Clerk     — authentication and subscription billing
  · Vercel    — global edge deployment

  BUSINESS MODEL
  ──────────────
  Free  — Start immediately, no card required
          4 invoices/mo · 10 buyers · 10 products · 3 emails/buyer
  Plus  — $9/mo  — Growing businesses
          100 invoices · 200 buyers · WhatsApp · AI extraction
  Pro   — $29/mo — Power users
          Unlimited everything · Zoho Books sync · 5 team seats

01. SMART INVOICE CREATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  Description
  ───────────
  Create detailed invoices with line items, GST rates, discounts
  (flat or percentage), and taxes — all computed live before saving.
  A full preview modal shows the final invoice layout before it's
  committed to the database.

  Problem Solved
  ──────────────
  Business owners waste time writing invoices by hand or in
  spreadsheets with no preview, leading to errors they only catch
  after sending.

  How It Works
  ────────────
  • Select a saved buyer → fields auto-fill (name, shop, GSTIN)
  • Add line items from your saved products catalogue
  • Set discount type (flat ₹ or %), GST/tax rate
  • Click "Preview Invoice" → review full breakdown
  • Click "Save Invoice" → stock quantities auto-decrement,
    buyer receives an email notification instantly

  Key Details
  ───────────
  • Subtotal → Discount → After-Discount → Tax → Total
  • Invoice number auto-generated
  • Supports INR and other currencies
  • Free plan: 4 invoices/month

02. AUTOMATED PAYMENT REMINDERS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  Description
  ───────────
  DueMate automatically sends payment reminders to buyers when
  invoices go overdue — without any manual action from the business
  owner. Reminders are sent daily at 11:00 AM IST for all overdue
  invoices, grouped by buyer into a single consolidated email.

  Problem Solved
  ──────────────
  Chasing payments manually is awkward and time-consuming. Most
  small businesses either forget to follow up or feel uncomfortable
  doing it repeatedly — leading to delayed cash flow.

  How It Works
  ────────────
  • Every day at 11 AM IST, a background job scans all invoices
  • Pending invoices past their due date are marked "Overdue"
  • One reminder email is sent per buyer listing all overdue bills
  • Overdue badges show red (>7 days) or amber (≤7 days)
  • Includes a "pay on time & save" incentive message

  Key Details
  ───────────
  • Powered by Inngest (durable background jobs — survives crashes)
  • Free plan: max 3 reminder emails per buyer per month
  • Slot 3 is always reserved for the overdue reminder
    (slots 1 & 2 used for invoice created + payment receipt)

03. INSTANT INVOICE & PAYMENT EMAIL NOTIFICATIONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  Description
  ───────────
  Every key transaction automatically triggers a professionally
  designed email to the buyer — no manual sending required.
  Three email types cover the full payment lifecycle.

  Problem Solved
  ──────────────
  Buyers often forget about invoices or aren't sure if their
  payment was received. Manual confirmation emails take time
  and often get skipped entirely.

  How It Works
  ────────────
  • Invoice Created  → "Invoice #001 from Your Business" email
    (line items table, totals, due date, GSTIN details)
  • Payment Recorded → "Payment Confirmed" receipt email
    (PAID/PARTIAL banner, amount, mode, date, reference)
  • Overdue          → "Friendly reminder" email with all
    outstanding invoices listed

  Key Details
  ───────────
  • Each email template has its own colour theme:
    Indigo (invoice) · Green (receipt) · Amber (reminder)
  • Owner's email is set as reply-to on every email
  • Email failure never blocks the payment save — fires async
  • Delivery tracked in notifications audit log

04. PAYMENT RECORDING & HISTORY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  Description
  ───────────
  Record partial or full payments against any invoice directly
  from the dashboard. Each payment is logged with its mode,
  date, amount, and optional reference/notes — building a full
  payment history per invoice.

  Problem Solved
  ──────────────
  Many buyers pay in installments. Without installment tracking,
  owners lose sight of how much is still owed and when payments
  were received.

  How It Works
  ────────────
  • Expand any invoice → click "+ Record Payment"
  • Enter amount (capped at balance due, amber warning if over),
    payment type (Cash / Online), reference ID, notes
  • Balance auto-updates; status flips to "Paid" when cleared
  • Full history of all payments visible in the invoice panel

  Key Details
  ───────────
  • Tracks paidCash and paidOnline separately
  • lastPaymentAt timestamp recorded per payment
  • Payment receipt email sent automatically (free plan: 1/month
    max to preserve slot for overdue reminder)

  • GSTIN validated against Indian format

    → all fields auto-fill instantly

06. PRODUCT CATALOGUE & INVENTORY TRACKING
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  Description
  ───────────
  Maintain a catalogue of products with selling rate, purchase
  rate, GST rate, HSN code, and live stock count. When a product
  is added to an invoice and saved, stock quantity automatically
  decrements.

  Problem Solved
  ──────────────
  Small businesses don't know their stock levels or margins
  in real time. They also retype product details on every invoice
  instead of picking from a saved list.

  How It Works
  ────────────
  • Add products with purchase rate, selling rate, unit, GST %
  • Purchase Total auto-computes (rate × qty × (1 + GST/100))
  • Margin % shown on each product card (selling vs purchase)
  • Stock badge: Green (good) · Amber (≤5 left) · Red (out)
  • Invoice save decrements stock server-side automatically

  Key Details
  ───────────
  • Free plan: up to 10 products total
  • Supports units: pcs, kg, g, litre, ml, box, dozen, metre, etc.

07. SUPPLIER MANAGEMENT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  Description
  ───────────
  Save your supplier details once — supplier name, shop name,
  phone, GSTIN — and auto-fill them when adding new products.
  No more retyping the same supplier information repeatedly.

  Problem Solved
  ──────────────
  Every time a business owner adds a product to their catalogue,
  they have to retype the supplier's shop name, phone, and GSTIN
  from scratch — even for products from the same supplier.

  How It Works
  ────────────
  • Add suppliers in the Saved Suppliers section (Products tab)
  • When adding a product, select a supplier from the
    "Auto-fill from Saved Supplier" dropdown
  • Shop name, phone, and GSTIN fill instantly
  • Override any field manually if needed
  • Edit saved suppliers inline at any time

  Key Details
  ───────────
  • Completely separate from the buyers system
  • Suppliers are private to each business account
